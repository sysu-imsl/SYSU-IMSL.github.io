HarmonyOS Courseware

HarmonyOS is a distributed operating system designed for multiple smart devices and all-scenario interactions, providing users with a seamless experience across different terminals. This course aims to provide learners with comprehensive and in-depth knowledge of HarmonyOS, covering everything from basic concepts to practical development aspects. Below are the main topics of this course:

  • 01 Introduction to HarmonyOS [Download]
  • 02 Kernel Design of HarmonyOS [Download]
  • 03 Process and Thread Model in HarmonyOS [Download]
  • 04 Process and Thread Scheduling in HarmonyOS [Download]
  • 05 Concurrency Control in HarmonyOS [Download]
  • 06 Memory Management in HarmonyOS [Download]
  • 07 Virtual Storage in HarmonyOS [Download]
  • 08 File System in HarmonyOS [Download]
  • 09 Input/Output Management in HarmonyOS [Download]
  • 10 Fundamentals of Application Development in HarmonyOS [Download]
  • 11 Fundamentals of Device Development in HarmonyOS [Download]
